Upcoming E-Waste Recycling Opportunities

Our spring e-waste recycling events are back! We'll be in all five boroughs this March, April, and May collecting unwanted electronics for free, responsible reuse and recycling. All participants will be eligible to enter a raffle for a Macbook Aircourtesy of Tekserve.
We will accept working and non-working computers, monitors, printers, scanners, keyboards, mice, cables, TV’s, VCR’s, DVD players, phones, audio/visual equipment, cell phones, and video gaming equipment. We do not accept any household appliances including microwaves and other kitchen appliances.
For more program and data security information, please check our FAQs. If you have additional questions, please email us or call (212) 477-4022.
